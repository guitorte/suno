# üéØ Introduction to Meta Tags

**Meta tags** are keywords that guide SunoAI in creating your music. They function as "creative instructions" that define genre, mood, instruments, effects, and song structure.

# ‚ö° How They Work

* **Drag & Drop**: Tags are automatically formatted with square brackets `[Tag]`
* **Form Lyrics**: Direct insertion into lyrics with automatic formatting
* **Style Field**: Used without square brackets as style descriptions
