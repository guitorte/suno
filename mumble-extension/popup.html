Suno Mumble Pinner

Always-on mumble toggle

Active on suno.com

The extension watches Suno's create page and forces the Mumble button to stay visible at all times — even when Suno would normally hide it based on your selected options.

💡 Open the browser console on suno.com and look for [Suno Mumble Pinner] log lines to confirm it's working.
